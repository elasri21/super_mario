How To Play

1 - You will walk Mario through to collect treasures

2 - Every time Mario gets a treasure , your score will increase by 1.

3 - red squares are obstacles, touching one will decrease your score by 1, the cost to remove it.

4 - black squares are enemies. touching one will decrease your lives by 1, the cost to kill it.

5 - grey squares are walls that stop Mario from moving
